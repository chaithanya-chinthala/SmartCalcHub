What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment made towards a loan. It consists of both the principal amount and interest. Using an EMI calculator helps you plan your finances better before taking a loan.

Why Use an EMI Calculator?

  • Understand your monthly payment instantly
  • Compare loan options easily
  • Plan your budget effectively
  • Visualize principal vs interest
