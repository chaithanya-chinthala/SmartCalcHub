What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a disciplined way of investing in mutual funds where you invest a fixed amount every month instead of a lump sum. It helps you build wealth gradually using the power of compounding.

How SIP Works

When you invest regularly, your money earns returns, and those returns are reinvested, helping your wealth grow faster over time.

Benefits of SIP

  • ✔ Disciplined monthly investing
  • ✔ Reduces market risk through averaging
  • ✔ Long-term wealth creation
  • ✔ Ideal for beginners and students
