What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is a grading system used in schools and universities to measure a student’s overall academic performance over a period of time.

How CGPA is Calculated

CGPA is usually calculated by averaging grade points obtained in all subjects. In many universities, it is converted into percentage using the formula:

CGPA × 9.5 = Percentage

Why CGPA is Important

  • ✔ Used for higher education admissions
  • ✔ Important in job applications
  • ✔ Standard academic performance measurement
  • ✔ Easy comparison between students
